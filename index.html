QR Code Cyber Threats

Understanding the risks to stay protected

Phishing Attacks

Malicious QR codes redirect users to fake websites designed to steal credentials, personal information, or financial data.

78% of QR phishing attacks target banking

Malware Distribution

QR codes can trigger automatic downloads of malicious software, ransomware, or spyware onto your device.

45% increase in QR malware in 2024

Payment Fraud

Fraudulent UPI QR codes redirect payments to criminal accounts instead of legitimate merchants.

₹12Cr lost to QR payment fraud annually

Privacy Invasion

Malicious QR codes can track your location, device information, and browsing habits without consent.

67% of malicious QRs collect personal data
