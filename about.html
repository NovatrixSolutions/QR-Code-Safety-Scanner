About CyberGuard QR Shield

Protecting users from QR code threats through advanced security technology

Our Mission

CyberGuard QR Shield was founded in 2023 to address the growing threat of malicious QR codes. As QR code usage exploded in payment systems, marketing, and authentication, cybercriminals quickly adapted to exploit this technology.

Our mission is to provide individuals and businesses with the tools they need to safely utilize QR technology without fear of falling victim to scams, fraud, or privacy invasion.
